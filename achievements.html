SYA conferences

SYA conferences are held annualy on topic issues affecting adolescents.The conferences are held in September.Admission is free and on school registration basis.Special conferences include future scientist conference for stydents interested in science,technology,engineering and mathematics(STEM).We believe in the adage"Catch them young" "Train up a child in the way he/she should go".Look out for the next conference coming at a location near you.
